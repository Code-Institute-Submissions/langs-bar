Our front main bar is what welcomes you upon entering the venue. A stylish, clean environment with comfortable seating and booths. As you progress further back into the venue you will be met with more seating, a dance floor and our smaller cocktail bar.

A wide variety of drinks are available from beers to cocktails. Please see our Menu's for more information on drinks.

Our brunch's are famous for hitting the spot with delicious bites and pornstar martini cocktail trees!

We have a very large variety of tasty cocktails available including pornstar martini cocktail trees.

To the rear of the venue you will find our garden. You will find comfortable lounge seating an outside bar, smoking area and outside events. Our outdoor seating area has heating and overhead protection so even if the weather does feel like partying, you can still carry on!
